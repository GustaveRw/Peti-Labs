Why PETI

Mental health issues/disorders are increasing rapidly in our African communities, and in other continents. Hence, more solutions to these issues are needed to be put in places.

However there are normal mental health facilities are here, numbers are continue to increase drastically. Due to inaccessiblity to those facilities, many people ended up taking wrong decisions about their lives.

There’s a need to address issues of getting mental health facilities, easy online therapy sessions (24-7, 365 days), an all inclusive financial network and easier remittance from diaspora to increase the purchasing power of the populations on the continent and all this in a technologically secure environment.

The above underscored the creation PETI.
